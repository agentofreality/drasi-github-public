## Getting Started

1. **Join our Discord** - Say hello in [#introductions](https://aka.ms/drasidiscord)
2. **Read the docs** - Understand what Drasi does at [drasi.io](https://drasi.io)
3. **Explore the code** - Start with [drasi-core](https://github.com/drasi-project/drasi-core) or [drasi-platform](https://github.com/drasi-project/drasi-platform)
4. **Find an issue** - Look for `good first issue` or `mentorship` labels
5. **Introduce yourself** - Comment on an issue you're interested in

## Expectations

- **Communicate regularly** - Weekly updates with your mentor
- **Ask questions early** - Don't stay stuck, reach out on Discord
- **Follow contribution guidelines** - See [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) and [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/drasi-project/drasi-platform/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)
- **Document your journey** - Blog posts and demos are encouraged!

## Tips for a Successful Application

- **Start contributing before applications close** - Show your interest through actions
- **Show genuine interest in the project** - Understand what Drasi does and why it matters
- **Quality over quantity** - One thoughtful PR beats five trivial ones
- **Engage with the community** - Be active on Discord and GitHub discussions
- **Read existing code** - Understand the codebase before proposing changes
